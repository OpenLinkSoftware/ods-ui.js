About ODS

The OpenLink Data Spaces are a powerful Cloud system to manage contacts, calendars, tasks, a social network, emails, bookmarks, news or blog feeds, and much more. ODS supports fine-grained sharing of information through complex access control lists. Its complete developer API and broad support of standard interfaces make it the perfect personal information management service, either deployed as a shared intranet or internet service or as a personal storage at home. ODS provides all the tools UI developers require to build professional and powerful cloud applications.

ODS is the powerful linked-data-enabled cloud platform for your data. Store profiles, addresses, calendar events, files, manage feeds, and protect everything with powerful ACL rules. Access your data through a comprehensive RESTful API or via established standard interfaces.
ODS supports a wide variety of registration and login mechnisms including WebID, OpenID, or OAuth-powered services like Facebook or Twitter.
ODS brings full profile management. Store your personal details, your likes, your dislikes, your achievements, manage your social network, and share details accoring to ODS' powerful ACL rules.
ODS provides full linked-data exposure for every piece of information stored within.
ODS features comprehensive ACL rules for any kind of data stored in the system. Share information with selected people, with groups of people, even with people whose profiles fulfill certain conditions. Thanks to WebID the people do not require an account with ODS. All they require is to authenticate themselves with their WebID.
ODS comes with the Briefcase, the file storage solution which matches all your needs:
  • The WebDAV interface allows managing your files through any compliant client.
  • The integrated ACL system allows to share files with other people or groups of people.
  • File annoations like tags or comments, or any other kind of property or relation can be attached to files thanks to the Linked Data store.
  • Mount other known web storage solutions like Google Drive or Dropbox directly into your ODS Briefcase and apply the same powerful ACL rules to them.
In addition to its comprehensive RESTful API which allows to manage every detail of the ODS system, ODS supports a variety of standard access protocols:
  • Access file via WebDAV.
  • Manage your address book via any CardDAV-compliant client.
  • Manage your calendar via any CalDAV-compliant client.
  • Retrieve the structured description of any resource in ODS by resolving its URI.
